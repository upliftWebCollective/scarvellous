About the business

Scarvellous! makes unique and innovative scarves that are a fusion of Western and Global styles.  The unique aspect is blending Global pattens and jewelry into the American fashion of scarves so that the products will match Western wear.  The innovative aspect is providing a mechanism for detacvhable jewelry, allowing the flexibility of the scarf to be worn with and without jewelry.  Welcome to Scarvellous! - American Trend, Global Touch".

Red

Red. Gold. Oh, how bold! The pattern on this scarf is called Banarasi, and is often coveted in India. It's easy to tell why. Red is always an attractive color, and the shimmering gold mango-work further accentuates it. To top it all off, a gorgeous golden necklace lies beneath the scarf. With its rich and antique finish, this design captures the regality of India.

Purple

Purple is commonly associated with royalty, which is exactly what the next design is fit for! Feel like a Rani [Queen] sipping tea in her courtyard in this elegant, violet scarf. The polished pearls give it a sophisticated, British Raj-era feel, while the brooch gives it that trademark Scarvellous! glimmer. In its entirety, this light and airy elegance leaves you wanting more.

BANDHANI

Greetings from Gujarat! Bandhani print is commonly seen in the Indian state of Gujarat. Similar to the American "tie-dye", this scarf can be thrown on with a pair of ripped jeans to give a laid-back appearance. The green choker provides a dash of femininity for a funky attire. What are you waiting for? Grab you backpack and hold on tight, because it's going to be one heck of a hayride with this Bandhani print boldness.

Black and Gold

It's tough to go wrong with black and gold--such a classic combination. With a short, golden necklace, and delicate golden embroidery, this one is the picture classiness. Go ahead and join Hollywood's elites. You'll fit right in. Just don't forget us little people!

Rainbow

Rainbow's all rage! Tap into your bubbly side with this ultra-cute, extra-fun, rainbow scarf. A spectacular silver-colored brooch serves as a universal complement to the array of colors present in this scarf. Don't be afraid to indulge!
